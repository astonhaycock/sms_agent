How It Works

1

You Send an SMS

Text your question in natural language—no commands, no syntax. Works via satellite SMS when you're off-grid.

2

Smart Routing

The system detects your intent and routes to the right tool: Weather API, Local LLM, Web Search, or First-Aid decision tree.

3

Intelligent Processing

High-confidence answers come from the local LLM. Low-confidence or fact-heavy queries trigger server-side web search with AI summarization.

4

Compressed Reply

Get a concise, actionable answer optimized for satellite bandwidth. Reply "more" for additional details if needed.
